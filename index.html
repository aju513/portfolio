About The Company

At Scrub and Polish Cleaning Services, we specialize in providing reliable commercial cleaning for businesses of all sizes. Whether you need daily maintenance or a periodic deep clean, our team has the expertise to get the job done efficiently and effectively.

What began in 2018 as a small residential cleaning service has now grown into a trusted name with over 1,000 satisfied clients. Over the years, we have expanded into commercial spaces, introduced eco-friendly solutions, and launched specialized deep-clean packages — all while staying committed to professionalism and care.

  • Specialized in commercial cleaning
  • Flexible cleaning plans
  • Advanced cleaning tools
  • Safety-first protocols
  • Eco-friendly, non-toxic products
